Key Improvements:
1. Global Controls (Start/Stop) moved to the top for immediate access.
2. Total Progress bar moved to the top level.
3. Phase Actions ([P]ause, [S]kip, [R]esume) appear ONLY in the active panel header.
4. No space wasted at the bottom of the screen.
